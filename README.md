## 📱 Features

### 🛒 Your Store, Anywhere

- **Daily Offers & Night Market**: Check your store rotation without launching the game.
- **Bundle Highlights**: View current bundles and their exact VP costs.
- **Wishlist**: Add your dream skins to a wishlist and get notified the moment they appear in your shop.
- **Shop Analytics**: Track your spending and shop history over time.

### 📊 Comprehensive Career Tracker

- **Player Profiles**: Search any player and view their Competitive rank, RR, and account level.
- **Advanced Metrics**: Dive deep into your stats—ACS, ADR, KAST%, Headshot percentages, and Win Rates.
- **Match History**: Detailed breakdown of every recent match, including round-by-round kill feeds, economy, and scoreboards.
- **Radar Charts**: Visualize your performance across different metrics instantly.

### 🎒 Loadout & Collection Manager

- **Browse Collection**: View all the skins, buddies, and player cards you own.
- **Custom Presets**: Create, save, and equip entire custom loadout presets with a single tap.
- **Equip on the Go**: Change your equipped skins, variants, and gun buddies directly from your phone.

### 📖 The Ultimate Encyclopedia

- **Agents**: Explore abilities, voice lines, and lore for every agent.
- **Weapons & Maps**: View detailed damage stats, spray patterns, and interactive map layouts.
- **Esports Hub**: Keep up with the VCT! View live match scores, upcoming schedules, and tournament brackets.

### ⚡ Power Tools

- **Instalocker**: Be the fastest in the lobby. Automatically lock in your main agent the second you find a match.
- **Live Match Scout**: See the ranks, stats, and match history of everyone currently in your lobby before the game even starts.
